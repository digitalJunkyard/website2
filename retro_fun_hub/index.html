Non-NLP Search Engines

Old School Search Engine

  • Straight-up word matching—no mind games.

  • Results like a raw mixtape—unfiltered, unexpected, sometimes messy, always real.

  • You’re in the driver’s seat, cruising the wild web.

  • Hidden gems, weird websites, the deep cuts.

Modern Search Engine

  • “I think you meant this…” AI, guessing, assumptions, and distractions.

  • They let you see what they want you to see- mainstream media.

  • You’re riding shotgun while the algorithm steers you where it wants.

  • Big brands and ads. Tracking you and selling you out.

Mojeek

A truly independent search engine that does not use AI ranking like Google. It focuses on exact keyword matching and doesn't prioritize results based on popularity.

Wiby

A search engine dedicated to indexing small, personal websites reminiscent of the early web. It won’t always return mainstream results.

Marginalia Search

Specializes in surfacing "forgotten" parts of the internet by ranking pages without bias toward popularity or SEO tactics. Great for discovering obscure or independent websites.

Gigablast

An old-school search engine that still offers raw keyword-based results, though it has a smaller index.

SearxNG

A meta-search engine that can be configured to return less-filtered results, including exact keyword matches.
