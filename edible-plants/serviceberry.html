Brief Description

The serviceberry, also known as Juneberry or Amelanchier, is a small deciduous tree or shrub native to North America. It produces small, edible berries that ripen in early summer and are prized for their sweet flavor and versatility in cooking.

Harvest times: Early summer.

Taxonomy

Family: Rosaceae

Genus: Amelanchier

Species: Various species within the Amelanchier genus

Types

There are several species of serviceberry, including:

  • Amelanchier alnifolia (Saskatoon serviceberry)
  • Amelanchier canadensis (Canadian serviceberry)
  • Amelanchier laevigata (Smooth serviceberry)
