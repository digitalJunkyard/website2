Brief Description

The everbearing mulberry is a fruit-bearing tree known for its sweet, juicy berries that can be harvested multiple times throughout the year. It is a popular choice for home orchards and edible landscapes.

Harvest times: Spring, Summer, and Fall.

Taxonomy

Family: Moraceae

Genus: Morus

Species: M. nigra (Black Mulberry), M. alba (White Mulberry), M. rubra (Red Mulberry)

Types

There are several popular types of everbearing mulberry, including:

  • Black Mulberry (M. nigra)
  • White Mulberry (M. alba)
  • Red Mulberry (M. rubra)
