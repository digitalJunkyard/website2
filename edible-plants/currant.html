Brief Description

Currants are small, berry-bearing shrubs belonging to the genus Ribes. They produce clusters of edible berries that range in color from red to black and are known for their tart flavor. Currants are commonly used in jams, jellies, sauces, and baked goods.

Harvest times: Late spring to early summer.

Taxonomy

Family: Grossulariaceae

Genus: Ribes

Species: Various species within the Ribes genus

Types

There are several species of currants, including:

  • Ribes rubrum (Red currant)
  • Ribes nigrum (Black currant)
  • Ribes usda (Buffalo currant)
