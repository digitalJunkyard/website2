Brief Description

A persimmon is a sweet, flavorful fruit that ripens in the fall. It is known for its orange color and unique taste.

Harvest times for hardiness zones 7b to 8a: October to November.

Taxonomy

Family: Ebenaceae

Genus: Diospyros

Species: D. virginiana (American persimmon), D. kaki (Asian persimmon)

Types

There are several popular types of persimmon, including:

  • American Persimmon (D. virginiana)
  • Asian Persimmon (D. kaki)
