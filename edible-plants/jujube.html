Brief Description

The jujube is a small, sweet fruit with a taste similar to dates. It is often dried and eaten as a snack or used in cooking. The jujube tree is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in arid climates.

Harvest times: Late summer to early fall.

Taxonomy

Family: Rhamnaceae

Genus: Ziziphus

Species: Z. jujuba

Types

There are several popular types of jujube, including:
