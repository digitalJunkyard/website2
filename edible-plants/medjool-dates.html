Brief Description

The Medjool date is a large, sweet fruit with a rich, caramel-like flavor. It is often referred to as the "king of dates" due to its size and taste. Medjool dates are commonly eaten fresh or dried and are popular in desserts and snacks.

Harvest times: Late summer to early fall.

Taxonomy

Family: Arecaceae

Genus: Phoenix

Species: P. dactylifera

Types

Medjool dates are typically classified by their size and quality, rather than distinct types.

However, variations in flavor and texture may occur based on growing conditions and cultivation methods.
