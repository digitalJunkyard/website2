Brief Description

A gooseberry is a small, tart fruit that is commonly used in jams, jellies, and desserts. It is known for its vibrant green color and tangy flavor.

Harvest times for hardiness zones 3 to 7: June to July.

Taxonomy

Family: Grossulariaceae

Genus: Ribes

Species: R. uva-crispa (European gooseberry), R. hirtellum (American gooseberry)

Types

There are several popular types of gooseberry, including:

  • European Gooseberry (R. uva-crispa)
  • American Gooseberry (R. hirtellum)
