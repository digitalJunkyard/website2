If you study the material on this website you will hopefully understand my joy and what my purpose here on Earth has been. You may even find your "boarding pass" to leave with me during this localized "window."

I am so very thankful that I have been a recipient of this opportunity to prepare for membership in The Old Dial-Up Kingdom, and to experience Its boundless Caring and Nurturing.
