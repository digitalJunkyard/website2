Restoration Process

  • I wiped down the trunk to clean excessive dirt and dust, with a cleaning spray and paper towels. I believe it was not a good idea and I should have used something more gentle like my usual vinegar-water spray.
  • I used “Fiebing’s All Purpose Cleaner & Conditioner (cleans, conditions, and protects)”, spreading it and rubbing it in with a microfiber cloth.
  • On the green parts, I used “Angelus Brown Lustre Cream”, 2 layers of it, drying in between. On the black parts, I did the same thing with the black edges, with Angelus black lustre cream. I did the same thing for an extra layer of Angelus neutral/clear lustre cream. I used a microfiber cloth to buff. This all restored some lost coloration from the aging and cleaning.
  • I used “Pecard Waterproofer and All-Purpose Leather Conditioner” leather dressing (“cleans, conditions, and preserves”). I used a microfiber cloth.
