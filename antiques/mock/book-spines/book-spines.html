Overview

Basically, I have 4 DVD cases in my living room. 3 of them hold games and 1 holds movies. I wanted them to look like antique books sitting there.

Printing and Preparing

  • I created the bookspine images with Gemini. I specified what I wanted them to say, the size, and the color.
  • I downloaded the images and cropped them.
  • I printed them out and used school glue to paste them on. Then I covered the whole thing in school glue on the outside to waterproof it.
