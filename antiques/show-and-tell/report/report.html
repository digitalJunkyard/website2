Purchase Info

This school report was bought by me, Kalyn Morris, on in March of 2026. It was bought at Antiques at Hall Lumber Co in Chester, SC (106 Hudson St, Chester, SC 29706).

Initial Assessments & Dating

  • Dennison's paper fasteners/ Brads. These were sold roughly 1897-1923.
  • Written with a typewriter with blue-ish ink. typewritten school reports began appearing in classrooms in the 1920s as typewriters expanded beyond offices.
  • I'm expecting a small overlap, pretty much 1920.
  • Written by G.A. Wentz.
