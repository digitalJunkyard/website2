Wells Fargo Money Orders

Purchase Info

I bought these Wells Fargo money order receipts in eBay. I thought it was fitting since I work at Wells Fargo.

Initial Assessments & Dating

The post card in the background is antique but I don't remember the date and I don't want to undo my framing.

The backdrop is red velvet, and the frame is thrifted.

I don't understand the wacky way they wrote dates, but I can see that the year is '07. Ah yes, the year of Avril Lavigne, Lincoln Park, Maroon 5, Good Charlotte. Soulja Boy and Umbrella haunted us. Oh wait, this was NINETEEN O' SEVEN?

This was the year of.. uhh.. On with the Play, Auld Lang Syne, Because of you, etc. Looks like someone named Bill Murray had a few hits. Source

"KEEP IT." the money order demanded, and J.D.Something Taylor took this VERY seriously.

One is for $5.00 and one is for $0.80. I've never personally sent a money order worth 80 cents. Google says $0.80 was worth $27.68, and $5.00 was worth $173 to $178. The other 2 say $19.90(.40?) and $2.00.

You'd think people would write more clearly when there's money involved.

This process would be safer than mailing cash. Basically you'd go to WF and give them the amount of money you want to send, plus a small fee. WF would mail it or carry it to the recipient's local bank.
