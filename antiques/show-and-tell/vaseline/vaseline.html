Vaseline Jars

Purchase Info

This school report was bought by me, Kalyn Morris, at Spence's Bazaar in Dover, DE (one of my hometowns).

Initial Assessments & Dating

  • The jars say "Vaseline" (in quotes) Chesebrough New-York.
  • Machine-made, screw-top lids for Vaseline started in 1906.
  • Amber jars started in 1896, for Carbolated Vaseline.
  • I'm gathering that the amber color of this particular jar would be 1909-1915.
